Hotels, Resorts, and Lodging

Location, Location, Location

The stunning beauty of our natural surroundings will reduce stress immediately. With many different attractions such as active volcano's, lavish jungles, crystal clear water and beachs Taniti is a sight to see. Islands are slow and far away from many distractions which makes them ideal for any get away or retreat. Taniti also offers many packages specifically for business travel.

Hotels, Resorts, and Villas

Taniti offers a wide range of hotels, resorts, villa's and off the grid housing. You will find everything you need here including island packages, available discounts, and travel tips to get your started.

Fact of the Day

Taniti has an indigenous population of about 20,000. Until a recent increase in tourism, most the Tanitian economy was dominated by fishing or agriculture.
